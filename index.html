ABOUT ME

Since I was very young I was very interested in technology, first in electronics, I liked to disassemble electronic objects and see how they worked inside.

Years went by and I decided to study a career in electronics, I started working in the electronics field and programming caught my attention, I started with microcontrollers and so I could combine my two passions, electronics and programming.

I had the opportunity to work as a software developer in the electronics industry, programming test equipment for electronic cards (Functional Tests), and automation of production lines with PLC.

I've been studying Web programming for 1 year, and since the first day I've been excited with this world, and waiting for the opportunity to work in this fascinating area, for the moment I'm in the Front-End area and little by little I'm getting into the Back-End.
